Research Interests

He started his research experience with reactions of N-heterocyclic carbenes (NHCs) including annulation reactions, and then enantioselective amidation reactions. For the BS project, he chose to expand the scope of nucleophiles to peroxides which could be intercepted by Bicyclo[1.1.0]butanes to access highly functionalized cyclobutanes. He is currently exploring other research areas within the realm of organic chemistry which gives the flavour of computational studies.
